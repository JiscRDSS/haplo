h1. Home page

h2. Noticeboard

The noticeboard displays the most recent ten notices. 

To add a new notice:
* scroll to the bottom of the noticeboard
* choose _Add item_
* enter the details
* _Save_.

To view more details about a notice click on ... at the end of the title. 

h2. Checked out files

Any checked out files will display on the homepage. 

h2. Quick links

To add a quick link:
* choose _Add link_ at the end of the list of quick links
* enter the details
* _Save_.

To view a quick link, click on the name of the link. To view more details about the quick link, click on the bullet point for each link.
