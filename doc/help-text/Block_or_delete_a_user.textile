h1. Block or delete a user

To block or delete a user go to the user's account in system management, and choose _delete_ or _block_.

h2. Blocked users

Blocked users are intended for temporarily removing access privileges to the system.

You can unblock an account by going to the _Users_ list in systems management and choose _Blocked_ to show all blocked users. Choose the user you wish to undelete from the list and choose _Unblock_ from the right hand panel. 


h2. Deleted users

Information about deleted users is kept by the system to maintain historical information.

You can undelete an account by going to the _Users_ list in systems management and choose _Deleted_ to show all deleted users. Choose the user you wish to undelete from the list and choose _Undelete_ from the right hand panel.
