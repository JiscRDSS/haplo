h1. Billing and usage

View the billing and usage page from the _Tools_ menu to see your current usage and limits:

* users include all active users (but not blocked users) 
* items are the records in the system, such as person, organisation, file or book
* storage is the overall storage required for your system shown in GB.

Your billing details are shown. Follow the instructions to modify your account.
